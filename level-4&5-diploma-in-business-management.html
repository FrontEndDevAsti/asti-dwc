Learning outcomes

Market Research Analyst

Market research analysts utilize data analytics to prepare reports on various business aspects, particularly financial ones. They analyze different markets and trends to develop strategies that enhance their firm's profitability. These analysts often collaborate with departments such as marketing and finance.

Business Risk Analyst

Business risk analysts help organizations assess and analyze financial risks associated with business activities. Using mathematical and statistical tools, they evaluate potential opportunities and suggest policies to mitigate identified risks. They create projections and models based on company data to inform decision- making.

Project Manager

Project managers oversee project teams to ensure the success of a product or service in specific areas. They conduct internal reviews to identify improvements for products, coordinate team efforts, and manage project progress to meet objectives.

Business Development Managers

Business development managers drive business growth within an organization. They build a network of contacts to attract new customers, explore emerging market opportunities, and manage growth initiatives. Their responsibilities also include projecting sales and estimating revenue to align with anticipated income.
