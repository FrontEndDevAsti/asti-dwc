Certificate In Leading The Internal Quality Assurance Of Assessment Processes AND Practice

The aim of this unit is to develop learners' ability to understand and apply the context, principles, planning, and maintenance aspects of internal quality assurance in assessment. By the end of this learning process, learners should be able to comprehend the context and principles that underpin internal quality assurance, demonstrate knowledge of planning internal quality assurance activities, and understand how to effectively maintain and improve the quality of assessment internally.

Course Overview

The aim of this unit is to develop learners' ability to understand and apply the context, principles, planning, and maintenance aspects of internal quality assurance in assessment.

By the end of this learning process, learners should be able to comprehend the context and principles that underpin internal quality assurance, demonstrate knowledge of planning internal quality assurance activities, and understand how to effectively maintain and improve the quality of assessment internally.

Course Objective

ULO1- Able to produce a work plan for own area of responsibility Understanding the Scope of Responsibility, Setting Clear Objectives and Goals, Identifying Key Deliverables and Milestones, Time Management and Prioritization, Risk Assessment and Mitigation, Communication and Reporting.

ULO2- Able to allocate and agree responsibilities with team members. Understanding Team Roles and Skills, Defining Project or Task Objectives, Matching Tasks to Team Members' Strengths, Involving Team Members in Allocation Process, Feedback and Performance Evaluation, Team Building and Collaboration.

ULO3- Able to review and amend plans of work for own area of responsibility and communicate changes. Plan Review Process, Performance Evaluation, Gathering Feedback, Data Analysis and Evaluation, Amendment Criteria, Problem-Solving and Decision Making, Understanding and Buy-In, Tracking Progress Post-Amendment, Documentation and Record Keeping
